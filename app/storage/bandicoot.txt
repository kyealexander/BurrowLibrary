Title: The Southern Brown Bandicoot
Scientific Name: Isoodon obesulus

The bandicoot is a small, nocturnal marsupial that digs cone-shaped holes in search of worms, grubs, and fungi.
Its burrowing helps aerate soil and spread plant seeds, making it an important little gardener of the bush.
